The BlueLeaf Foundation™ offers donors a unique NFT in return for their contribution to the Seatrees organization, which is dedicated to combating climate change. The process is fully transparent, secure, and traceable, thanks to blockchain technology, and every donation has a measurable impact. To trace your donation, visit the sea or tree smart contract. At the moment everything runs on the sepolia blockchain, a testnet of ethereum.

FAQ

I have no idea what this website does. Can you explain it to me?
This website lets you donate to protect rainforests. You connect your digital wallet (like MetaMask), choose how much to donate, and in return, you get a unique digital collectible (NFT). The whole process is secure and transparent, thanks to blockchain technology.
Which type of wallet can I use?
Unfortunately, only MetaMask is supported at the moment. Here you can download it.
I see only two pictures—how is every NFT unique?
Each NFT has its own unique Token ID stored on the blockchain. Even if the images are the same, the Token ID ensures every NFT is distinct and traceable.
At the moment this contract runs only on testnetworks - Where can I get the test tokens?
You can get test tokens for the sepolia testnet here. Or you can just lool up in the browser for sepolia faucet.
I made everything right, but I didn't reveive my NFT. What can I do?
Make sure that in your settings on metamask in privacy and security "Automatically detect NFTs" is enabled; otherwise, you can try to import your NFT thrght the TokenID and the Tree or Sea contract. As last option, contact me!
